The Best Movies According to Angie

My Top 3 Movies

The Count of Monte Cristo

The movie follows Edmond Dantès, a young sailor who is falsely accused of treason by his jealous friend Fernand Mondego.
Imprisoned in the Château d'If, Edmond befriends a fellow inmate who helps him escape and reveals the location of a hidden treasure.
After escaping, Edmond finds the treasure, assumes the identity of the Count of Monte Cristo, and sets out to exact revenge on those who wronged him.

Matilda

Matilda Wormwood is a brilliant young girl with telekinetic powers, mistreated by her neglectful parents and tyrannical school principal, Miss Trunchbull.
Finding solace in books and the kindness of her teacher, Miss Honey, Matilda uses her abilities to stand up against the injustices she faces at home and school.
Ultimately, she helps Miss Honey reclaim her life and home, and Matilda finds a loving family in Miss Honey.

V for Vendetta

This film explores themes of totalitarianism, rebellion, and the fight for freedom, drawing inspiration from historical and political contexts,
and is notable for its use of the Guy Fawkes mask as a symbol of resistance.
